<p><p>A panel filter is a type of air filtration device widely used in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) systems. It consists of a flat filter media enclosed within a frame, designed to capture dust, allergens, and other airborne particles, thereby improving indoor air quality and enhancing system efficiency. The demand for panel filters is primarily driven by increasing awareness of air quality and stringent regulations concerning environmental standards.</p><p>The Panel Filter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.8% during the forecast period. Technological advancements, such as the development of high-efficiency particulate air (HEPA) filters and electrostatic filters, are also playing a crucial role in market evolution. Additionally, the burgeoning trend of smart buildings and automation in HVAC systems is propelling the demand for advanced filtering solutions.

<p><strong>The Rotary Wheel Heat Exchangers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Condensing Rotary Heat Exchanger</li><li>Sorption Rotary Heat Exchanger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotary wheel heat exchangers are classified mainly into two types: condensing rotary heat exchangers and sorption rotary heat exchangers. Condensing rotary heat exchangers efficiently recover waste heat from exhaust gases, utilizing it to preheat incoming air, enhancing energy efficiency in HVAC systems. In contrast, sorption rotary heat exchangers utilize desiccant materials to absorb moisture from the air, improving humidity control and overall air quality. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing energy efficiency and sustainability in various applications.</p></p>

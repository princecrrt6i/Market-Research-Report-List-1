<p><p>Automatic flash point testers are vital instruments used to determine the ignition temperature of flammable liquids. The market includes two primary types: open cup flash point testers and closed cup flash point testers. Open cup testers expose the sample to air, allowing for a broader range of applications, particularly in testing liquids with higher flash points. In contrast, closed cup testers provide a controlled environment, making them ideal for measuring lower flash points with greater safety and accuracy.</p></p>

<p><p>The electric power tools market is primarily divided into two types: corded and cordless. Corded tools are powered directly from an electrical outlet, offering consistent power and performance, ideal for heavy-duty tasks. They are typically more powerful but limited by the length of the cord. Cordless tools, on the other hand, operate on rechargeable batteries, providing greater mobility and convenience without the hassle of cords. They are suitable for lighter tasks and outdoor use, making them increasingly popular among consumers and professionals alike.</p></p>

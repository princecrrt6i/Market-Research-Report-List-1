<p><strong>The Shopping eGift Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Loop</li><li>Closed-Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shopping eGift card market can be classified into two main types: open-loop and closed-loop. Open-loop eGift cards are issued by financial institutions and can be used at multiple retailers, providing flexibility to consumers. In contrast, closed-loop eGift cards are specific to a single retailer or brand, allowing purchases only within that particular store or chain. While open-loop cards offer broader spending options, closed-loop cards often come with retailer-specific promotions and benefits to enhance customer loyalty.</p></p>

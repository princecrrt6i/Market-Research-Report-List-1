<p><p>The single-component acrylic adhesive market is categorized into water-based, solvent-based, and other formulations. Water-based adhesives utilize a polymer emulsion, offering low volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions and environmental benefits, making them ideal for indoor applications. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents, providing strong bonds and quick curing times, suitable for industrial uses. Other types may include hot melt or UV-curable adhesives, tailored for specific applications that require unique properties like flexibility or rapid curing under light exposure.</p></p>

<p><p>A non-contact laser distance sensor is a device that uses laser technology to measure distances without physical contact with the target object. These sensors work by emitting a laser beam that reflects off the target and returns to the sensor, allowing for precise distance measurements. They are widely used in various industries, including manufacturing, construction, automotive, and robotics, due to their accuracy and reliability.</p><p>The Non-contact Laser Distance Sensor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.80% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for automation and precision measurement across various sectors.

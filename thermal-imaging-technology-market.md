<p><strong>The Thermal Imaging Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uncooled Type</li><li>Cooled Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal imaging technology markets are primarily divided into two types: uncooled and cooled thermal imaging systems. Uncooled systems rely on infrared radiation to detect temperature variations without the need for cooling, making them compact and cost-effective, ideal for various applications such as surveillance and HVAC. In contrast, cooled thermal imaging systems utilize cryogenic cooling to enhance sensitivity and provide higher image resolution, making them suitable for defense, aerospace, and high-performance industrial applications where precision is critical.</p></p>

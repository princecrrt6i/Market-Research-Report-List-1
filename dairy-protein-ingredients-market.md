Whey protein concentrates and isolates, known for their high bioavailability, are popular in sports nutrition. Whey protein hydrolysates are pre-digested for rapid absorption, making them ideal for recovery products. Casein and caseinates, slowly digesting proteins, are used in various applications, including cheese production and nutritional supplements, offering sustained amino acid release.</p></p>

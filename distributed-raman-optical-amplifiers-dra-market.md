<p><p>Distributed Raman Optical Amplifiers (DRA) enhance signal strength in optical communication by utilizing the Raman scattering effect. The Pre-Raman market focuses on amplifying signals before transmission, improving performance over long distances. Conversely, the Post-Raman market amplifies signals after they have been transmitted through the fiber, compensating for losses incurred during transmission. Both types aim to enhance signal quality and extend communication ranges, catering to different stages of the optical transmission process in telecommunications networks.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Hand-in</li><li>Hand-under</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hand drying machine market can be categorized into two main types: hand-in and hand-under dryers. Hand-in machines require users to place their hands inside a designated cavity where air is blown to dry them, providing an enclosed and efficient experience. In contrast, hand-under dryers rely on an exposed design, requiring users to hold their hands underneath a nozzle that directs drying air. Both types prioritize hygiene and convenience, catering to diverse consumer preferences and bathroom layouts.</p></p>
